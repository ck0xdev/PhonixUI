Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At PhonixUI, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our open-source CSS framework.

1. Information We Collect

PhonixUI is a static, open-source CSS framework. We do not require you to create an account, provide personal data, or log in to download and use the phonixUI.min.css file. If you browse our documentation website, standard server logs (such as IP addresses, browser types, and access times) may be collected automatically by our hosting provider for security and analytics purposes.

2. Use of Information

Any standard analytics information collected is used solely to maintain the quality of the website, analyze usage trends, and improve the documentation. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking

The PhonixUI documentation website may use essential cookies to ensure the site functions correctly (for example, remembering if you prefer a specific documentation layout). You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites, primarily GitHub. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any website you visit.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the maintainer via the PhonixUI GitHub repository.
